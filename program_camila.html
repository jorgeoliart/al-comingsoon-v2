Your custom English program: Guiding Star

Hello Camila, I'm glad to begin this learning journey with you. I have designed a program that reflects Astral Lingua's personalised and humanistic approach. We want to help you build your confidence for authentic communication.

The plan

This is our map for the next ten sessions. Our main goal is to help you feel more confident and comfortable speaking and understanding English, so you can use it to connect with others in a way that feels true to you.

Our approach for this program is:

  • Authentic and meaningful: We will try to connect what we learn to your life, your experiences, and your passions.
  • Focused on communication: The most important thing is connection. We will practice speaking and listening in a relaxed and supportive way. It's a safe space to try new things and make mistakes.
  • Built on confidence: A core part of our work will be building your confidence and helping you feel capable and in control of your learning.

Our 10-session journey

Here is a look at what we will explore in each session. This is a flexible plan, and we can adapt it to your progress and interests.

Session 1: My world, my people

We’ll start by talking about the important people in your life. You'll practice describing your friends and family, making sure you feel confident introducing the people you care about to others.

Session 2: My life in Urubamba

Let's talk about your life right now! We will explore how to describe some of your routines, what you like to do in this beautiful town, and what is happening around us in the present moment.

Session 3: Sharing your stories

When you travel, you experience great stories. In this session, we will begin to explore the past. You will practice telling the story of your journey to Peru and sharing memories from your travels.

Session 4: This or that? Making commparisons

We will learn how to compare things, people, and places. You'll be able to share your opinions and explain your preferences.

Session 5: A walk & talk detour

For this special session, we will leave the cafe and take a short, easy walk. We will practice English in the real world, describing the nature and urban elements around us.

Session 6: Looking ahead: my future plans

Where will your journey take you next? We will focus on how to talk about your future plans, dreams, and intentions, whether for next week or next year.

Session 7: A look back: my other life

You have an interesting professional background! We'll talk about your past work, practice talking about a familiar topic, and share another part of your story.

Session 8: Life's big experiences: "Have you ever...?"

A fun session where we share our life experiences. You will learn how to ask and answer questions about travel, food, and people.

Session 9: People in Peru

Let's focus on making meaningful connections. We’ll practice describing the interesting people you are meeting and learn how to start conversations.

Session 10: Your path forward

In our final session, we will celebrate your progress, and create a simple, personal plan for you to continue learning and practicing English on your own.

Ready to Begin Your Journey?

This plan is our map, but our conversations will be the real adventure. Remember, the goal is for you to express your true self with confidence. I am here to support and guide you on every step of your path.
